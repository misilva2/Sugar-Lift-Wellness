About Us

Sugar Lift Wellness is a nurse-led wellness studio focused on education, coaching, and sustainable diabetes management for rural families.

  • Clinical expertise

    Evidence-based guidance from a Registered Nurse with Type-1 diabetes lived experience.

  • Community access

    Workshops and virtual visits designed for rural communities with limited resources.

  • Whole-person care

    Nutrition, movement, mindset, and stress management are all part of the plan.

Our Story

Tiffany Silva was diagnosed with Type-1 diabetes and built a practice rooted in empathy, education, and empowerment. Sugar Lift Wellness began with a mission to bridge healthcare gaps in rural communities—bringing trusted guidance to families who need it most.

Mission

To educate and help rural communities with sustainable diabetes management through compassionate, culturally aware care.

Vision

A future where every family has access to practical tools, coaching, and confidence to live well with diabetes.

Services

Flexible programs designed for individuals, families, and community partners.

  • Personal coaching

    One-on-one support sessions covering nutrition, CGM insights, and lifestyle routines.

  • Group education

    Community workshops with practical strategies for daily diabetes care.

  • Care plan audits

    Comprehensive reviews of existing health plans with actionable upgrades.
