About Tiffany

Tiffany Silva is a Medicare-certified registered nurse and certified diabetes educator with a BSN from Grand Canyon University. As Director of Nursing at Cochise Eye and Laser in rural Arizona, she witnessed firsthand how healthcare workforce shortages and limited specialty access leave diabetes patients without essential education and support.

Diagnosed with type 1 diabetes in her early twenties, Tiffany experienced the challenges of navigating a complex healthcare system with limited rural resources. These dual perspectives—as both clinician and patient—inspired her to found Sugar Lift Wellness, directly addressing the barriers that prevent rural communities from accessing certified diabetes education.

Through telehealth services, bilingual education programs, and community partnerships, Tiffany is working to eliminate the gaps in care that lead to preventable complications and poor outcomes in underserved areas.

Credentials & Certifications
