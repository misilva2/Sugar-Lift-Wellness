About Tiffany

Tiffany Silva is a registered nurse with a BSN from Grand Canyon University. She serves as the Director of Nursing at Cochise Eye and Laser, leading teams with compassion and precision. Diagnosed with type 1 diabetes in her early twenties, Tiffany has refined daily management methods that balance nutrition, stress, and routine. She now brings those insights to the community through Sugar Lift Wellness, helping families in rural areas feel supported, informed, and empowered.
